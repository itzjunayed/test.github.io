About

Md. Shakib Shahariar Junayed is a Bangladeshi computer science researcher and software developer with a strong focus on deep learning, medical image segmentation, and computer vision. He is currently pursuing his Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering at North South University (NSU), Dhaka, where he has also served as a Research Assistant in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science.

Junayed has co-authored several peer-reviewed publications in the field of medical imaging, including works on: Lumbar spine MRI segmentation using deep learning and data enhancement techniques. Semantic segmentation of multiple sclerosis lesions integrating Mamba Sequence Models and hierarchical upsampling networks. Prostate zonal segmentation and Circle of Willis anatomical segmentation using advanced neural architectures. ResearchGate His research has been cited in both academic and clinical contexts, reflecting growing recognition in the medical AI community.

Technical and Professional Roles

Professionally, Junayed is a Junior Software Developer at Tanvir Constructions Ltd. He also contributes as a Full-Stack Developer for the IEEE NSU Student Branch's online platform. Additionally, he has held leadership roles such as Secretary (External) and Lead Ambassador for the IEEE NSU Student Branch, where he has been actively involved in student professional development initiatives.
